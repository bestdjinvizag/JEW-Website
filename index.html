Meet the Founder

Founded by Vasanth Jayanthi in Visakhapatnam, our event management company is built on strong technical and creative expertise. This background helps us deliver events with proper planning, safety, and reliable execution.

Today, our professional team manages corporate events, product launches, weddings, birthdays, society celebrations, and DJ shows. We focus on smooth coordination, quality service, and audience-friendly performances to make every event memorable.
